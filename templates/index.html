Welcome to Programming Herd Mentality!

In this game, you'll answer programming-related questions. Points are awarded to players who give the same answer as the majority. The goal is to think like the herd!

Remember: Good programmers think alike! 🐮

🥛

How to Play

  1. Enter the names of all players in your herd
  2. Choose how many rounds you want to play
  3. Each player takes turns answering the programming question
  4. Points are awarded to players who match the majority answer
  5. The player with the most points at the end is the lead cow!
